# FinTrack / ProjetSprintJava

A modern Java-based financial management platform designed to simplify and centralize personal financial activities through an intuitive and secure desktop application.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

FinTrack is a FinTech application developed as part of an integrated engineering project.  
The platform helps users efficiently manage their financial ecosystem by centralizing:

- Account management
- Portfolio management
- Budget tracking
- Products & subscriptions management
- Financial document management

The goal of FinTrack is to provide users with better financial visibility, smarter budgeting, and a more organized digital financial experience.

---

## ✨ Main Features

### 👤 Account Management
- Secure user authentication
- Profile management
- Financial account handling

### 💼 Portfolio Management
- Monitor balances and assets
- Organize financial portfolios
- Track account performance

### 📊 Budget Management
- Budget planning
- Expense tracking
- Financial analytics and statistics
- Dynamic alerts and notifications

### 🛒 Products & Services Management
- Manage subscriptions and services
- Product tracking
- Centralized service management

### 📂 Document Management
- Upload financial documents
- Organize invoices and reports
- Secure document access
